# Italy — National Technology Strategies (8 Domains)

*Source: Gemini deep research, 2026. Country 18 (Batch 4). OECD/EU bloc.*

Italy's tech transformation is driven by the €190B+ **PNRR (National Recovery and Resilience Plan / NextGenerationEU)**, especially Mission 4 (Education & Research), using a "Hub and Spoke" governance model linking academia, public research bodies (ENEA), and industry. Strong alignment with EU frameworks (Chips Act, Critical Raw Materials Act, AI Act). Historic reversal: returning to nuclear after the 1987 referendum ban.

## 1. Nuclear Energy
- **Plan:** National Platform for a Sustainable Nuclear Power (PNNS / Piattaforma Nazionale per un Nucleare Sostenibile)
- **Body:** Ministry of Environment and Energy Security (MASE), with ENEA and RSE
- **Year:** 2023–2024 (conclusive reports Oct 2024)
- **URL:** https://www.mase.gov.it/portale/-/piattaforma-nazionale-per-un-nucleare-sostenibile-pubblicazione-rapporti-conclusivi
- **Summary:** A historic reversal of the post-Chernobyl 1987 referendum ban, evaluating nuclear's reintroduction by 2050 for decarbonization and energy security. Expressly rules out legacy Gen-III large plants, focusing instead on SMRs, Gen-IV Advanced Modular Reactors (e.g. lead-cooled fast reactors), and long-term fusion. The platform's 7 working-group reports coordinate research institutes, universities, and industry to build a supply chain and address social acceptability.

## 2. Artificial Intelligence
- **Plan:** Italian Strategy for Artificial Intelligence 2024–2026 (Strategia Italiana per l'IA)
- **Body:** Agency for Digital Italy (AgID), Department for Digital Transformation (Presidency of the Council)
- **Year:** July 2024
- **URL:** https://www.agid.gov.it/sites/agid/files/2024-07/Strategia_italiana_per_l_Intelligenza_artificiale_2024-2026.pdf
- **Summary:** Released just after the EU AI Act, drafted by a 14-expert committee. Four macro-areas: Research, Public Administration, Enterprises, and Training — with a network of facilitators to drive SME adoption and AI-driven PA automation. Aligns with the EU AI Act; envisions specialized Italian large models. Backed by the PNRR FAIR (Future AI Research) Extended Partnership mobilizing 350+ researchers across 10 hubs. (Italy ranks ~7th globally in AI publications but struggles with tech transfer.)

## 3. Quantum Information Science
- **Plan:** Italian Strategy for Quantum Technologies (Strategia Italiana per le Tecnologie Quantistiche)
- **Body:** MUR (Ministry of University and Research), with MIMIT, DTD, MAECI, Defense, ACN
- **Year:** September 2024
- **URL:** https://www.mur.gov.it/it/strategia-italiana-le-tecnologie-quantistiche
- **Summary:** A roadmap for leadership in quantum computing, simulation, sensing, and secure communication. Anchored by the PNRR-funded National Center for HPC, Big Data and Quantum Computing (ICSC) at the Bologna Tecnopolo — which integrated a 140-qubit Pasqal neutral-atom computer with the pre-exascale Leonardo supercomputer (hybrid classical-quantum). Focuses on deep-tech startups, quantum sensing, and migrating to post-quantum cryptography for national security.

## 4. Biomedical Sciences / Biotechnology
- **Plan:** PNRR National Center for Gene Therapy and RNA-Technology Drugs + Bioeconomy BIT II Implementation Action Plan (2025–2027)
- **Body:** MUR (RNA Center) / National Bioeconomy Coordination Board, Presidency of the Council (BIT II)
- **Year:** 2022–2026 (RNA Center); 2024 (BIT II IAP)
- **URL:** https://www.rna-genetherapy.eu/ ; https://cnbbsv.palazzochigi.it/media/gx3n4rwl/iap-16122024-en.pdf
- **Summary:** Dual-track: €320M+ PNRR hub-and-spoke center (Padua, Bambino Gesù) for mRNA vaccines, CRISPR, and gene therapies; plus the BIT II bioeconomy plan expanding sustainable biomanufacturing, blue (marine) biotechnology, and bio-based feedstock valorization toward a circular economy. Aims to make Italy a European leader in precision medicine.

## 5. Materials Science / Critical Raw Materials
- **Plan:** National Technical Table on Critical Raw Materials + Critical Raw Materials Decree
- **Body:** MIMIT (Ministry of Enterprises and Made in Italy) and MASE
- **Year:** 2023–2024
- **URL:** https://www.mimit.gov.it/it/impresa/competitivita-e-nuove-imprese/materie-prime-critiche/materie-prime-critiche
- **Summary:** Aligned with the EU Critical Raw Materials Act, establishes a permanent technical committee and a "National Exploration Program" to update Italy's dormant mining maps and assess domestic reserves (lithium, cobalt, rare earths). Strong emphasis on circular economy — "urban mining" / WEEE recovery and eco-design — to cut import dependence and strengthen strategic autonomy.

## 6. Semiconductors / Microelectronics
- **Plan:** National Microelectronics Plan (Italian Chips Act / Piano Nazionale per la Microelettronica)
- **Body:** MIMIT and MUR
- **Year:** 2023
- **URL:** https://www.mimit.gov.it (Italian Chips Act / microelettronica)
- **Summary:** Responds to the EU Chips Act (doubling EU share to 20% by 2030) with ~€700M in augmented R&D tax credits and FDI attraction — exemplified by Silicon Box's €3.2B advanced-packaging/backend facility in Novara (Piedmont). Establishes the National Center for Semiconductor Integrated Circuit Design in Pavia to link academic research with industry.

## 7. Digital Technology / Digital Infrastructure
- **Plan:** Italia Digitale 2026
- **Body:** Department for Digital Transformation (MITD/DTD), Presidency of the Council
- **Year:** 2021 (implementation to 2026)
- **URL:** https://assets.innovazione.gov.it/1665677773-italia-digitale-2026.pdf
- **Summary:** One of the largest PNRR commitments (~€13.4B) split between ultra-broadband and PA digital transformation. 2026 targets: nationwide 1 Gbps connectivity, 75% cloud adoption among public administrations, and 70%+ of the population using digital identity (SPID/CIE). Flagship "Polo Strategico Nazionale" (PSN) sovereign cloud hosts ministry/local-government data; the "PA Digitale 2026" platform helps small municipalities migrate and integrate pagoPA.

## 8. Autonomous Systems
- **Plan:** PNRR Extended Partnerships — MICS (Made in Italy Circolare e Sostenibile) + FAIR (Future AI Research)
- **Body:** MUR (Ministry of University and Research)
- **Year:** 2022–2026
- **URL:** https://www.mics.tech/ ; https://www.unibo.it/en/research/nrrp-opportunities-expectations-and-results/nrrp-projects-2013-extended-partnerships
- **Summary:** No single standalone drone/AV document; autonomy is embedded in PNRR Extended Partnerships fusing AI, robotics, and manufacturing. MICS Spoke 8 funds AI-driven, collaborative-robotics "digital factories"; FAIR Spoke 5 (Sapienza) covers "High-quality AI in Physical Systems" (safe human-robot interaction). The Defense Digital Strategy 2026–2030 frames military autonomous/multi-domain systems. Aims to elevate "Made in Italy" via low-impact automation. (Note: no dedicated civil AV/drone national strategy identified.)
