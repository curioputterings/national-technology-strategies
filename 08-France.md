# France — National Technology Strategies (8 Domains)

*Source: Gemini deep research, 2026. Country 8 (Batch 2). OECD bloc.*

France pursues "souveraineté technologique et industrielle" via an "État stratège" (strategist state) model. The €54B **France 2030** plan (managed by SGPI) is the primary engine across nearly all domains, bridging basic research (PEPR programmes) with industrialization. Strongly pro-nuclear, and tightly synchronized with EU frameworks (Chips Act, IPCEI).

## 1. Nuclear Energy
- **Plan:** Programme Nouveau Nucléaire / Plan Relance Nucléaire (within France 2030); Programmation pluriannuelle de l'énergie (PPE 3, 2026–2035)
- **Body:** Presidency, Ministry of Energy Transition, Conseil de Politique Nucléaire (CPN), SGPI, Délégation interministérielle au nouveau nucléaire (DINN)
- **Year:** 2021 (France 2030); 2022 (Belfort speech); 2024 (CPN); PPE 3 adopted by Décret n° 2026-76 (12 Feb 2026); EPR2 Bugey PIG decree (24 Mar 2026)
- **URL:** https://www.legifrance.gouv.fr/jorf/id/JORFTEXT000000202676
- **Summary:** Historic nuclear revitalization: build six next-gen EPR2 reactors (studies for eight more) for low-carbon independence by 2050. €1B ring-fenced under France 2030 for SMRs/AMRs, prioritizing the EDF-led NUWARD project; CPN backs ~10 domestic SMR/AMR startups. The long-delayed multi-year energy programming framework (PPE 3, 2026–2035) was finally adopted by decree in February 2026, legally committing to the EPR2 fleet and lifting caps on extending existing reactors' lifespans. On 24 March 2026 a decree (n° 2026-213) qualified the twin-EPR2 project at Bugey (Ain) as a "projet d'intérêt général," with EU state-aid review of the fleet's financing opened ahead of a final investment decision targeted for end-2026. First EPR2 targeted for ~2035; ASN/ASNR oversees safety.

## 2. Artificial Intelligence
- **Plan:** Stratégie Nationale pour l'Intelligence Artificielle (SNIA), Phases 1, 2 & 3 (post-AI Action Summit)
- **Body:** Directorate General for Enterprise (DGE), Ministry of Economy; SGPI; Ministre déléguée chargée de l'IA et du Numérique
- **Year:** 2018 (Phase 1, Villani Report); 2021/22 (Phase 2); 2023 (GenAI committee); 2024 (IA Booster); Feb 2025 (Paris AI Action Summit); 2025–26 (Phase 3 / "Pionniers de l'IA")
- **URL:** https://www.entreprises.gouv.fr/priorites-et-actions/autonomie-strategique/
- **Summary:** Aims to make France a top global AI hub and safeguard European digital sovereignty. Phase 1 (~€1.5–1.85B) built research capacity (3IA institutes, Jean Zay supercomputer); Phase 2 (~€1.1–1.5B under France 2030) focuses on economic dissemination — embedded, frugal, trustworthy and generative AI. France hosted the Paris AI Action Summit (Feb 2025), catalyzing ~€109B in announced private investment and a "trusted, accessible, sustainable AI" agenda. A "Phase 3" deployment push followed: the France 2030 "Pionniers de l'IA" call (run via Bpifrance/Inria) named its first 23 laureates (≈€10M) in February 2026 with a second wave at VivaTech in June 2026, channeling sovereign AI into healthcare, cybersecurity, robotics and the ecological transition. Supports the EU AI Act.

## 3. Quantum Information Science
- **Plan:** Stratégie Nationale pour les Technologies Quantiques (incl. PROQCIMA)
- **Body:** SGPI, DGE, Ministry of Higher Education & Research, CEA, CNRS, Inria
- **Year:** January 2021 (launch); March 2024 (PROQCIMA / acceleration)
- **URL:** https://www.pepr-quantique.fr/la-strategie-nationale-pour-les-technologies-quantiques/
- **Summary:** ~€1.8B (public + private) initially, plus €1B more in 2024 under France 2030, across six pillars (computers, simulators, sensors, post-quantum crypto, secure comms, enabling tech). Upstream research via PEPR Quantique (CNRS/CEA/Inria); 600+ publications, 90+ labs. Flagship PROQCIMA: a state-procurement competition tasking five startups (Alice & Bob, Pasqal, Quandela, Quobly, C12) to deliver two French fault-tolerant quantum computers (1,024 logical qubits) by 2032.

## 4. Biomedical Sciences / Biotechnology
- **Plan:** Plan Innovation Santé 2030 (Acceleration Strategy: Biothérapies et bioproduction)
- **Body:** Presidency, Ministries of Research & Health, SGPI, CEA, Inserm
- **Year:** June 2021 (plan); January 2022 (biotherapies strategy)
- **URL:** https://www.economie.gouv.fr/strategie-france-leader-production-therapies-innovantes
- **Summary:** €7.5B to make France Europe's leading health-innovation nation. Core pillar: €800M for Biotherapies and Bioproduction, to reverse ~95% dependency on imported biopharmaceuticals. Targets ≥20 new biomédicaments produced on French soil, doubling sector employment to 20,000, and world-class bioclusters. Three axes: Biotherapies (€800M), Digital Health (€650M), Emerging Infectious/CBRN threats (€750M); research via PEPR BBTI (CEA/Inserm).

## 5. Materials Science
- **Plan:** PEPR DIADEM (emerging materials) + PEPR Recyclage (circular economy / critical materials); Plan national de résilience « Terres rares et aimants permanents »
- **Body:** CNRS, CEA, SGPI (France 2030), Ministry of Ecological Transition; Ministry of Economy / Industrial Sovereignty
- **Year:** 2022 (DIADEM); 2023 (Recyclage); 5 May 2026 (Plan résilience Terres rares)
- **URL:** https://presse.economie.gouv.fr/article/la-france-engage-la-reconquete-de-sa-souverainete-industrielle-sur-les-terres-rares-et-les-aimants-permanents/
- **Summary:** Multi-pronged. DIADEM (€85M) uses AI, high-throughput screening, and robotics to cut materials discovery-to-market from ~20 years to 4–10 (a French "materials genome"), targeting energy/mobility/electronics. PEPR Recyclage (€40M, within a €300M+ strategy) targets recycling of five critical material classes to secure battery and renewable supply chains. In May 2026 the government added a dedicated national resilience plan for "Terres rares et aimants permanents" to confront ~85% dependence on foreign rare-earth processing: it extends the France 2030 "Métaux critiques" call to 2027, expands the C3IV green-industry tax credit through 2028 for mining and recycling, mobilizes state guarantees for raw-material traders, and (via an April 2026 decree) sets the investment doctrine of the Fonds Métaux critiques — framed as a national-security imperative for permanent magnets used in EVs, wind power and defense.

## 6. Semiconductors / Microelectronics
- **Plan:** Stratégie pour l'électronique (France 2030) / Nano 2030 → Semi-conducteurs 2035 (in finalization)
- **Body:** Directorate General for Enterprise (DGE), Ministry of Economy
- **Year:** July 2022 (electronics strategy); 22 May 2026 (€550M call / "Semi-conducteurs 2035")
- **URL:** https://www.elysee.fr/emmanuel-macron/2026/05/22/forum-europeen-sur-la-puissance-de-calcul-les-sciences-et-technologies-quantiques-et-les-semi-conducteurs
- **Summary:** Aligned with the EU Chips Act; the original €5B push aimed to double domestic semiconductor capacity by 2030, prioritizing low-power FD-SOI, power electronics (GaN, SiC) and advanced sensors, anchored by the €7.5B STMicroelectronics–GlobalFoundries "mega-fab" in Crolles (Isère). In May 2026 the government opened a new €550M France 2030 call and confirmed France's participation in the EU IPCEI on Advanced Semiconductors (PIIEC AST), announcing a fully revised national strategy — "Semi-conducteurs 2035" — to succeed the 2022 electronics acceleration strategy and steer investment through 2035. Uses the IPCEI ME/CT framework for state aid; next-gen R&D via CEA-Leti.

## 7. Digital Technology / Digital Infrastructure
- **Plan:** Stratégie d'accélération Cloud + Stratégie nationale réseaux du futur (5G/6G)
- **Body:** DGE, DINUM (Interministerial Digital Directorate), SGPI
- **Year:** 2021 (launch); 2023/24 updates
- **URL:** https://www.numerique.gouv.fr/ ; https://pepr-futurenetworks.fr/
- **Summary:** Cloud strategy (€1.8B total, €667M public) builds a sovereign cloud via the SecNumCloud security visa (ANSSI-certified, immune to extraterritorial laws like the US CLOUD Act) and a "Cloud au centre" default for government IT. Future Networks strategy (€735M public) deploys 5G industrial use-cases, sovereign telecom (Open RAN), and 6G R&D (PEPR Future Networks). Both driven by data-sovereignty concerns against US hyperscalers and Asian telecom vendors.

## 8. Autonomous Systems
- **Plan:** Stratégie Robotique et machines intelligentes
- **Body:** SGPI (France 2030), Ministry of Industry, CNRS, ANR
- **Year:** October 2021 (announcement); 2023/24 project calls / PEPR
- **URL:** https://c-robotique-france2030.org/
- **Summary:** €800M under France 2030 to reindustrialize France via robotics, autonomous systems, and smart machines — domain-agnostic (industrial cobots, aerial/terrestrial/maritime drones, agri-robotics, service robots). Fundamental research via PEPR O2R (Organic Robotics, €30M, CNRS-led) on mobility, perception, manipulation, and frugal embedded AI. Commercialization via "Défi transfert robotique" (up to €10M/project), framing automation as the enabler of decarbonized domestic manufacturing (subject to EU DNSH principle).
