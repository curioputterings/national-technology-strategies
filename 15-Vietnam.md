# Vietnam — National Technology Strategies (8 Domains)

*Source: Gemini deep research, 2026. Country 15 (Batch 3). ASEAN bloc.*

Vietnam is making a state-directed pivot from labor-intensive assembly to an innovation-driven economy, driven from the highest Party levels (Politburo resolutions, esp. Resolution 57-NQ/TW on S&T&I) emphasizing "Make in Vietnam" core technologies. Capitalizes on the "China Plus One" shift. Notable reversal: the 2016 nuclear moratorium has been lifted, reviving the Ninh Thuan projects.

## 1. Nuclear Energy
- **Plan:** Revised National Power Development Plan VIII (Decision 768/QD-TTg) + Atomic Energy Law (94/2025/QH15) + Resolutions 174/2024/QH15 and 189/2025/QH15 (restart + special mechanisms)
- **Body:** Prime Minister; National Assembly
- **Year:** Restart resolution Nov 2024; Amended PDP8 April 2025; Atomic Energy Law June 2025; special-mechanism Resolution 189/2025 (amended by Resolution 121/2026/UBTVQH15, Mar 2026)
- **URL:** https://www.trade.gov/market-intelligence/vietnam-revised-power-development-plan-viii
- **Summary:** Officially reverses the 2016 moratorium, reclassifying nuclear as a "green" strategic baseload for energy security and decarbonization (JETP). Commits ~US$22B to revive Ninh Thuan 1 and 2, targeting 4,000–6,400 MW operational between 2030–2035 and ~8,000 MW added by 2050, mobilizing SOEs EVN and PVN under IAEA-aligned regulation. Resolution 189/2025/QH15 grants special investment, compensation and resettlement mechanisms to accelerate the projects, later expanded by Standing Committee Resolution 121/2026/UBTVQH15 (March 30, 2026). Implementation accelerated in early 2026: an intergovernmental construction agreement was signed with Russia (Rosatom VVER-1200) on March 23, 2026, with resettlement construction for Ninh Thuan 1 slated to begin around June 2026. Driven by ~10%/yr electricity demand growth.

## 2. Artificial Intelligence
- **Plan:** National Strategy on Research, Development and Application of AI until 2030 (Decision 127/QD-TTg) + Law on Artificial Intelligence (Law No. 134/2025/QH15)
- **Body:** Prime Minister; National Assembly
- **Year:** Strategy 2021 (Jan 26, 2021); AI Law Dec 10, 2025 (effective Mar 1, 2026)
- **URL:** https://english.luatvietnam.vn/law-no-134-2025-qh15-dated-december-10-2025-of-the-national-assembly-on-artificial-intelligence-422299-doc1.html
- **Summary:** The 2021 Strategy aims to make Vietnam a top-4 ASEAN and top-50 global AI nation by 2030, targeting three national AI innovation centers, 10 regionally prestigious AI brands, interconnected HPC centers, and 50 open datasets. In December 2025 the National Assembly passed Vietnam's first comprehensive standalone Law on Artificial Intelligence (134/2025/QH15, effective March 1, 2026) — among the earliest binding national AI statutes in ASEAN (8 chapters, 35 articles). It installs a risk-based regime (high/medium/low) over the research, development, provision and use of AI systems, with transparency, explainability, safety, accountability and data-governance duties, plus phased compliance grace periods (18 months for finance/health/education, 12 for other sectors). It operationalizes the AI-governance provisions foreshadowed by the Law on Digital Technology Industry, anchoring AI as a strategic pillar of Resolution 57-NQ/TW.

## 3. Quantum Information Science
- **Plan:** Project on Research, Application and Development of Quantum Technology + Program for Excellent Basic Research (PEBR) 2026–2035
- **Body:** Politburo / Central Steering Committee; Ministry of Science and Technology (PEBR)
- **Year:** 2026
- **URL:** https://vietnamnet.vn/en/vietnam-launches-decade-long-push-for-ai-semiconductors-and-quantum-tech-2520111.html
- **Summary:** Elevates quantum to top-tier national security ("arrive later but not move slower"), aiming for independent quantum computing, communication, and cryptography for digital sovereignty. Uses PEBR to funnel stable long-term funding into specialized "research ecosystems," targeting 10+ core technology platforms by 2030. Heavy public-private leverage: FPT pledged US$100M for a Quantum AI Institute (100 PhDs, 500 patents by 2035), drawing diaspora talent.

## 4. Biomedical Sciences / Biotechnology
- **Plan:** Resolution 36-NQ/TW on Biotechnology Development and Application
- **Body:** Politburo of the Communist Party of Vietnam
- **Year:** 2023
- **URL:** https://vietnamlawmagazine.vn/politburo-resolves-on-biotechnology-application-...-70011.html
- **Summary:** Aims to make biotech an essential techno-economic sector contributing 10–15% of GDP by 2045, with a globally advanced level in agricultural, medical, and industrial biotech by 2030 (a top SE Asian "smart-production" hub). Targets food security, high-yield crops, biopharma self-reliance (reducing imported biological products), and preferential funding for high-value tech transfers; a rigorous biotech statistical framework by 2028.

## 5. Materials Science / Rare Earths
- **Plan:** Master Plan on Mineral Exploration, Mining, Processing and Use 2021–2030 / vision 2050 (Decision 866/QD-TTg) + Law on Geology and Minerals (54/2024/QH15)
- **Body:** Prime Minister; National Assembly
- **Year:** 2023 (Decision 866); 2024 (Law 54)
- **URL:** https://www.usasean.org/article/vietnam-approves-master-plan-exploration-exploitation-processing-and-use-mineral-resources
- **Summary:** Vietnam holds ~20–22M tonnes of rare earth reserves (2nd globally after China). The plan mandates domestic deep processing — strategic minerals licensed only to enterprises with advanced eco-friendly tech, explicitly restricting raw-ore exports. Expects ~US$18.6B investment by 2030 to unlock deposits in Lai Chau, Lao Cai, Yen Bai (~2M tonnes crude ore/yr), producing 99%-purity rare earth carbonate and integrating into the global "mine-to-magnet" chain.

## 6. Semiconductors / Microelectronics
- **Plan:** National Strategy for Semiconductor Industry Development through 2030, Vision 2050 (Decision 1018/QD-TTg) + Semiconductor HR Development Program (Decision 1017/QD-TTg)
- **Body:** Prime Minister
- **Year:** 2024 (September 2024)
- **URL:** https://english.luatvietnam.vn/cong-nghiep/decision-1018-qd-ttg-2024-...-366694-d1.html
- **Summary:** A "C = SET + 1" strategy (Chip = Specialized + Electronics + Talent + new destination) climbing from OSAT to IC design and localized fab. Three phases targeting 100+ design firms, 10 packaging/testing facilities, and one small-scale fab by 2030. Decision 1017 commits to training 50,000 semiconductor engineers and four national labs by 2030. Revenue ambitions: >US$25B/yr by 2030 and US$100B by 2050; up to 50% R&D subsidies to attract Amkor, Hana Micron, Intel.

## 7. Digital Technology / Digital Infrastructure
- **Plan:** Digital Infrastructure Strategy through 2025, orientation to 2030 (Decision 1132/QD-TTg) + Law on Digital Technology Industry (Law No. 71/2025/QH15)
- **Body:** Prime Minister; National Assembly
- **Year:** Strategy 2024 (October 2024); Law June 14, 2025 (effective Jan 1, 2026)
- **URL:** https://english.luatvietnam.vn/lawonthedigitaltechnologyindustryno71-2025-qh15datedjune142025ofthenationalassembly-405695-doc1.html
- **Summary:** Treats digital infrastructure as equivalent to roads/energy: 5G for 100% of high-tech zones and major administrative areas by 2025, expanding to 99% of the population by 2030, with ≥2 new international subsea cables by 2025 and 6 more by 2030 (addressing South China Sea connectivity vulnerabilities) and hyperscale, AI-ready green data centers. In June 2025 the National Assembly enacted the Law on Digital Technology Industry (71/2025/QH15, effective Jan 1, 2026) — Vietnam's first codified legal framework for the digital economy. It legally recognizes digital assets (including virtual assets/crypto) as property under the Civil Code, sets development parameters for the semiconductor and AI industries, authorizes regulatory sandboxes for emerging tech, and offers large incentives (up to ~37-year tax breaks for major projects). Together these underpin the goal of a digital economy reaching 30% of GDP by 2030.

## 8. Autonomous Systems
- **Plan:** National Unmanned Traffic Management System (Decision 808/QD-TTg) + Strategic Technologies & Products List (Decision 21/2026, updating Decision 1131)
- **Body:** Prime Minister / Deputy PM; Ministry of National Defence (UTM)
- **Year:** 2025–2026
- **URL:** https://en.vietnamplus.vn/vietnamese-uav-firms-showcase-capabilities-...-post342591.vnp
- **Summary:** Targets a multi-billion-dollar "low-altitude economy" — urban logistics, infrastructure monitoring, defense — classifying robotics, autonomous systems, and UAVs as strategic technologies. Tasks the Ministry of National Defence with a unified Unmanned Traffic Management (UTM) system enabling BVLOS operations. Aims to shift from importing (mostly Chinese) drones to mastering domestic flight-control algorithms, sensors, and 5G telemetry, supported by a Low-Altitude Economy Alliance.
